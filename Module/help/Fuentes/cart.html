Your shopping cart



This is the center of the application. From here you can access the different parts of it. Click on accommodations to see our range of hotels, apartahotels and motels. On the other hand, click on Parks to check the available theme parks. To see already prepared packs combining both, click on packages.



The center of the screen shows a list that will be updated as you make your purchases. Click just one time on an item to select it, and the options you have choosed will be shown on the right part of the screen. There you have the buttons Edit and Delete , to change the content of the selected reservation or to erase it.

The moment you add something to the cart, the button Confirm will be available,l to proceed with the reservation.
