Reservations of the accommodation



On this view, you can configure the various aspects concerning the reservation of this specific accommodation. There must be at least one person, child or adult. You must also specify a valid date, apart from the number of days that you will stay and whether you want a breakfast or not (beware that choosing that option will increase the price).

In addition, if you want to make this reservation more than once, you can do it with the option below breakfast.

Given that you have some kind of special request, feel free to indicate it on the space below.
